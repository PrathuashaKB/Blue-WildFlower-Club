Wildflowers embody a poetic essence. Although they grow freely in the wild, you’ll also find that they follow certain patterns. They stay rooted in the soil yet can be unpredictable. They possess a simple beauty. The same way we can't control the growth and freedom of wildflowers is the same way we can view loving people in our lives. You only want to ensure their freedom when you love rather than tame them. Lust, on the other hand, usually wants control and taming.
Imagine viewing your loved ones as wildflowers. They all look different, act differently, and have unique growth cycles. At the end of the day, to truly love is to allow another to blossom freely.
